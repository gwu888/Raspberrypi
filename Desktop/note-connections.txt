

How to connect Cap5 cable to outside?

1 - relay no 1 (Orange)
2 - black (ground)
3 - relay no 2 (Green)
4 - black (ground)
5 - relay no 3 (Gray)
6 - black (ground)
7 - Water meter Pulse (Brown)
8 - black (ground)


Power Supply:

Yellow: 12V -- connect to "Common" of all of 4 Relays
Red:    5V -- connect to Pi 5V
Black:  Ground - to all Cap5 Ground
